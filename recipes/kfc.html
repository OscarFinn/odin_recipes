Korean Fried Chicken Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of frosted cereal flakes
  • 1 chocolate bar, broken into pieces
  • 1 cup of spicy kimchi
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup of maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coffee
  • 4 glazed doughnuts, sliced in half for buns
  • 1 cup of whipped cream
  • A handful of fresh mint leaves

Directions:

  1. Prepare the coating: Crush the frosted cereal flakes into a fine powder and mix with broken pieces of the chocolate bar.
  2. Marinate the chicken: Combine the maple syrup, soy sauce, and ground coffee in a bowl. Marinate the chicken breasts in this mixture for at least 2 hours in the refrigerator.
  3. Fry the chicken: After marination, coat the chicken breasts thoroughly in the cereal and chocolate mixture. Deep fry until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Assemble the burger: Take a glazed doughnut half and layer with spicy kimchi. Place the fried chicken on top, add a dollop of whipped cream, and then garnish with fresh mint leaves. Top with the other half of the doughnut.
  5. Serve immediately: Enjoy this unique Korean Fried Chicken Burger with a side of disbelief and curiosity.
