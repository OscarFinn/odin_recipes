Pizza

A modern twist on the italian classic

Enjoy this culinary adventure that challenges the boundaries of traditional pizza!

Ingredients:

  • 1 large tortilla wrap (as the pizza base)
  • 1/2 cup of mint chocolate chip ice cream
  • 1/4 cup of ketchup
  • 2 bananas, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of marshmallows
  • 1 cup of popcorn
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of cinnamon sugar
  • 1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • A handful of gummy bears

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare the base: Lay the tortilla wrap on a baking sheet and brush it lightly with olive oil.
  3. Create the sauce: Instead of traditional pizza sauce, spread ketchup evenly over the tortilla. For an adventurous twist, add dollops of mint chocolate chip ice cream.
  4. Add the toppings: Distribute the banana slices, marshmallows, and popcorn over the sauce. Sprinkle cinnamon sugar across the top for a sweet and spicy flavor.
  5. Cheese it up: Cover the toppings with shredded mozzarella cheese, and for the grand finale, scatter a generous handful of gummy bears on top.
  6. Bake: Place the pizza in the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the edges are golden brown.
  7. Serve: Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve with an open mind and a sweet tooth!
